Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our services.

2. How We Use Cookies

ResumeSmartBuild uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: To enable core website features
  • Authentication: To keep you logged in securely
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website
  • Performance: To optimize website speed and functionality

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Third Party
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, security, and user authentication Session / Persistent Firebase (Google)
Preference Cookies Remember your settings, language preferences, and customizations Persistent (1 year) First party
Analytics Cookies Help us understand website usage and improve our services Persistent (2 years) Firebase Analytics
Performance Cookies Monitor website performance and user experience Persistent (1 year) First party

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database services
  • PayPal: Payment processing (when using subscription features)
  • Chatbase: Customer support chatbot functionality

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Browser Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

5.2 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we'll ask for your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences
  • Change your preferences at any time

6. Essential Cookies

Some cookies are essential for our website to function properly. These include:

  • Authentication tokens to keep you logged in
  • Security cookies to prevent fraud
  • Session cookies for basic functionality
  • Load balancing cookies for performance

These cookies cannot be disabled without affecting website functionality.

7. Analytics and Performance

We use analytics cookies to:

  • Count website visitors and traffic sources
  • Measure how users interact with our content
  • Identify popular features and content
  • Monitor website performance and errors
  • Improve user experience based on usage patterns

8. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we may use local storage technologies including:

  • Local Storage: To save user preferences and resume drafts
  • Session Storage: For temporary data during your visit
  • IndexedDB: For offline functionality and data caching

9. Mobile Applications

If you use our mobile applications, we may collect similar information through:

  • Device identifiers
  • App usage analytics
  • Crash reporting data
  • Push notification tokens

10. International Transfers

Some of our cookie data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice via email for significant changes
